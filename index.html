Yo Nigga, This How You Use It!

  1. Allow camera access on the browser, I wont spy on you
  2. Select front or back camera (mobile only)
  3. Enter your actual height for calibration "Not compulsory"
  4. Stand about 2 meters (6 feet) from the camera in a well-lit area
  5. Make sure your full body is visible in the frame
  6. Assume a T-pose (arms extended to the sides) for accurate measurements
  7. Click "Start Measurement" to begin capturing
  8. Click "Capture Photo" to take a snapshot with measurements
  9. Enter client name and download the results
  10. Viola! View your final results in the right panel
  11. Boom! Boom!
